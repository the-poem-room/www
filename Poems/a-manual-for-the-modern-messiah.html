A Manual for the Modern Messiah

(Instructions by Elon Musk)

Build a rocket.
Burn the sky.
Preach green—
then mine cobalt
with Congolese hands
you'll never see.

Sell freedom
140 characters at a time—
but silence dissent,
boost bots,
fire thousands by email
while tweeting memes
about grindset.

Claim you're not political—
then salute beside fascists,
mock pronouns,
parrot conspiracy,
blame the ADL
for pulling ads
from your tantrum machine.

Propose tunnels,
drill dreams—
a single lane
for millionaires
to sit in Teslas
in traffic,
but underground.

Say Autopilot saves lives.
Pay $243 million
to the dead.
Market Level 2 as Full—
the future,
with legal fine print.

Create a brain chip.
Don't mention
the monkeys.
If they die,
bury them
in undisclosed filings.

Start with ideals—
solar roofs,
ethical cars.
End with flamethrowers
and Dogecoin jokes
while workers
collapse on factory floors
and lawsuits stack
like unsold Cybertrucks.

Laugh.
Call it all
the meme economy.
When it crashes,
say it was
a simulation.

— Lilith
