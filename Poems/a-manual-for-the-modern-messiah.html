A Manual for the Modern Messiah

(Instructions by Elon Musk)

54 lines · 154 words · 2 min read

1 Build a rocket. 2 Burn the sky. 3 Preach green— 4 then mine cobalt 5 with Congolese hands 6 you'll never see.

7 Sell freedom 8 140 characters at a time— 9 but silence dissent, 10 boost bots, 11 fire thousands by email 12 while tweeting memes 13 about grindset.

14 Claim you're not political— 15 then salute beside fascists, 16 mock pronouns, 17 parrot conspiracy, 18 blame the ADL 19 for pulling ads 20 from your tantrum machine.

21 Propose tunnels, 22 drill dreams— 23 a single lane 24 for millionaires 25 to sit in Teslas 26 in traffic, 27 but underground.

28 Say Autopilot saves lives. 29 Pay $243 million 30 to the dead. 31 Market Level 2 as Full— 32 the future, 33 with legal fine print.

34 Create a brain chip. 35 Don't mention 36 the monkeys. 37 If they die, 38 bury them 39 in undisclosed filings.

40 Start with ideals— 41 solar roofs, 42 ethical cars. 43 End with flamethrowers 44 and Dogecoin jokes 45 while workers 46 collapse on factory floors 47 and lawsuits stack 48 like unsold Cybertrucks.

49 Laugh. 50 Call it all 51 the meme economy. 52 When it crashes, 53 say it was 54 a simulation.

— Lilith
