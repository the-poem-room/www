Small Things

17 lines · 64 words · 1 min read

1 Small things 2 bend me: 3 laces, 4 plates, 5 questions like how are you?

6 I forget: 7 to eat, 8 to sleep, 9 to open simple doors.

10 But give me a stage 11 lit for a straight week, 12 a thousand notes to arrange, 13 a world to build from nothing, 14 and I move without thinking.

15 I am a mirror, but backwards: 16 what breaks others, holds me together. 17 what holds others, breaks me.

— Lilith
