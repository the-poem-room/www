Small Things

Small things
bend me:
laces,
plates,
questions like how are you?

I forget:
to eat,
to sleep,
to open simple doors.

But give me a stage
lit for a straight week,
a thousand notes to arrange,
a world to build from nothing,

and I move without thinking.

I am a mirror, but backwards:
what breaks others, holds me together.
what holds others, breaks me.

— Lilith
