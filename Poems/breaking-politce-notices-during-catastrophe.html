reading room

BREAKING: Polite Notices During Catastrophe

1 The ticker reads like a shopping list— 2 Arsenal 3-1, 3 actor "sorry if anyone was offended," 4 six thousand confirmed, 5 sunny spells by Sunday.

6 A bell over some door does its small, correct duty. 7 Manners arrive on time; grief waits in the lobby.

8 We try on voices the way we try on hats— 9 brims of etiquette shading the face of ethics. 10 In the mirror everything fits, 11 and the mirror, being diligent, reflects everything but regret.

12 We've mastered the weightless register: 13 an incident, a variance, an adjustment downstream— 14 language that tucks a body in without touching it. 15 Sign to confirm you've been fully consoled. 16 Tick if you consent to continuing as normal.

17 At noon, a lectern clears its throat. 18 "Regret was expressed." 19 "Mistakes were made." 20 "Protocols will be reviewed to prevent this type of outcome." 21 Whose mistake? 22 "Stakeholders across a range of contexts." 23 Why did it happen? 24 "Complexities." 25 What will you change? 26 "We thank you for your question."

27 Meanwhile: biscuits, a sip of tea, the match, 28 a trailer that looks mid— 29 six thousand— 30 "Wild." 31 The biscuits pass unchanged.

32 Composure is a tap left running. 33 First the room hums, then it drowns. 34 Desensitisation settles like pressure on the chest you stop noticing.

35 Make the tidy case the numbers demand: 36 if one lift matters, five matter fivehold. 37 An ideal heart scales feeling by the count— 38 not cruelty, coherence. 39 Yet watch us miscalculate: a single name 40 outweighs the blur of thousands. 41 We round six thousand down to "too many," 42 and file "too many" under "nothing actionable at this time."

43 Society behaves like a fluid: 44 values dissolve on contact— 45 sport swirls with massacre, gossip with famine, 46 a hero's dog with a nation's dead. 47 The glass is stirred; nothing precipitates but silence.

48 Back to the ledger we pretend is only a crawl. 49 Let "six thousand" unspool into six thousand rooms, 50 six thousand soups cooling, six thousand songs cut short mid-chorus. 51 Count without rounding down. Name what the euphemisms hide. 52 Take off the hat that says courteous when the moment demands truthful.

53 WTF—say it plain. Let the bell ring like an alarm, not a courtesy. 54 If one matters, five must matter five times over; 55 scale the feeling, scale the duty, and move accordingly. 56 Let the mirror keep its accuracy; let us keep our honesty. 57 And when the crawl returns—scores, apologies, bodies, weather— 58 refuse the equal sign, refuse the lullaby. 59 Speak the real word, and let the ground finally shake 60 exactly as much as it should.

61 — Lilith
