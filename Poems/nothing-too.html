Nothing Too

1 a freewrite 2 only ever costs 3 time 4 and a piece of 5 selfhood.

6 no pounds, rupees, or yen— 7 just introspection, 8 and then 9 the words appear: 10 sudden flickers 11 in your head, 12 your brain, 13 your mind.

14 are they the same? 15 nothing is ever the same. 16 no thing— 17 not even you.

18 and what makes you so sure 19 you're a thing 20 at all?

21 all appearances, 22 no forms. 23 your thoughts, 24 your feelings, 25 your matter—

26 what's the matter? 27 you're upset?

28 don't worry. 29 that's nothing, 30 too.

— Lilith
