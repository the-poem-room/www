Nothing Too

a freewrite
only ever costs
time
and a piece of
selfhood.

no pounds, rupees, or yen—
just introspection,
and then
the words appear:
sudden flickers
in your head,
your brain,
your mind.

are they the same?
nothing is ever the same.
no thing—
not even you.

and what makes you so sure
you're a thing
at all?

all appearances,
no forms.
your thoughts,
your feelings,
your matter—

what's the matter?
you're upset?

don't worry.
that's nothing,
too.

— Lilith
