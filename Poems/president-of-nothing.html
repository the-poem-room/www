President of Nothing

1 Begin with a tower. 2 Make it gold. 3 Make it loud. 4 Make it lie. 5 A monument to yourself, 6 hollow as your handshake. 7 Six bankruptices, 8 two impeachments, 9 zero accountability. 10 You sold a dream, 11 then sued the dreamers. 12 You called yourself a builder, 13 then built debt and drama, 14 stiffed the workers, 15 cooked the books, 16 and called the wreckage "deal-making."

17 You cried law and order 18 while dodging the draft, 19 paying hush money to porn stars, 20 and stoking riots 21 with your thumb. 22 You held a Bible 23 like it burned. 24 You gassed peace 25 for a photo. 26 You wept for statues— 27 but not for children 28 in cages 29 you ordered.

30 You said: 31 "I alone can fixt it." 32 "Very fine people on both sides." 33 "Stand back and stand by." 34 "Grab 'em by the—" 35 "We love you. You're very special." 36 "I won by a lot." 37 Every word a brand. 38 Every lie a strategy. 39 Every silence a scream.

40 You mocked the weak, 41 the grieving, 42 the faithful, 43 the fallen. 44 You turned cruelty 45 into applause. 46 You called truth fake 47 and made grievance 48 a religion. 49 You governed by gut, 50 and your gut was rotten.

51 You led a nation 52 through a plague 53 with bleach, 54 contempt, 55 and golf. 56 Half a million gone— 57 you blamed masks, 58 China, 59 Fauci— 60 never yourself. 61 You couldn't bear silence. 62 Needed claps like breath. 63 An empty room 64 was death.

65 You watched January 6th 66 like it was ratings night. 67 Glass shattered. 68 Flags fell. 69 Five dead. 70 And still you smiled. 71 You summoned a mob 72 to stop the count, 73 called it democracy, 74 called it love.

75 You drained nothing 76 but the last of our trust. 77 Sold sneakers, 78 steaks, 79 NFTs, 80 pardons. 81 You appointed judges 82 to unwrite the century. 83 Overturned rights, 84 shrank protections, 85 stacked courts 86 like casinos rigged to your odds. 87 You gave power 88 to the past.

89 And when the indictments came— 90 for fraud, 91 for sexual abuse, 92 for sedition— 93 you called them hoaxes, 94 called the courts corrupt, 95 called yourself the chosen, 96 called your lawyers.

97 No jobs returned. 98 No coal revived. 99 No swamp drained. 100 No peace made. 101 No truth told. 102 No shame shown. 103 No apology, 104 no humility, 105 no country 106 unchanged.

107 Only slogans. 108 Only echoes. 109 Only hats. 110 Only you, 111 in the mirror, 112 pretending it was ever real.

113 President of Nothing.

— Lilith
