Twelve Pounds and a Dream

Jump to stanza

1 We gathered the world's greatest minds — 2 and gave them a budget of twelve pounds. 3 They asked for resources. 4 We gave them synergy workshops. 5 They asked for data. 6 We offered an 'inspirational quote' 7 screen-printed on a tote.

8 The think tank was a rusted tank. 9 The briefing came handwritten 10 on the back of a Tesco receipt. 11 It smelled faintly of despair.

12 Someone asked for change. 13 We jingled coins in our pockets 14 and said, "We're working on it."

15 The forests burned. 16 We launched a hashtag campaign — 17 #EcoVibesOnly, with a baby panda 18 and a QR code that linked to merch. 19 The oceans rose. 20 We held brunch on a rooftop terrace, 21 posted it with the caption 'Rise Above.'

22 Someone mentioned the children. 23 We dimmed the lights for dramatic effect. 24 Ran a PowerPoint in italics. 25 Rebranded it 'Vision 2030.' 26 Applauded the clipart.

27 Someone proposed feeding the poor. 28 We issued a cvoucher 29 for one (1) smile 30 and a slightly bruised avocado.

31 Morale hit an all-time low. 32 We launched a podcast, 33 brought in a mindfulness goat, 34 in a government-issued suit.

35 Someone asked what the future looks like. 36 We passed around a mirror, 37 framed in recycled plastic, 38 blamed the lighting, the government, 39 then each other — 40 and called it progress.

— Lilith
