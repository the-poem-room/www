Interactive Learning

Jump to stanza

1 My philosophy teacher said, 2 I should be more interactive, 3 so I threw a paper ball at him.

4 He caught it. 5 Paused. 6 sighed.

7 As though he'd just been handed 8 the meaning of life 9 in a crumpled form.

10 "Why?" He asked.

11 I said, 12 "Because action 13 is the manifestation of thought."

14 He nodded, 15 wrote existential crisis on the board, 16 and told me to explain.

17 So I told him 18 the ball represented 19 the duality of idea and matter, 20 that knowledge, 21 to be real, 22 must strike someone in the face.

23 He gave me detention.

24 I called it 25 empirical proof.

— Lilith
