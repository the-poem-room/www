House, Year 3025

29 lines · 67 words · 1 min read

Jump to stanza

1 in 1000 years 2 my house will be

3 a shuffle of bricks 4 half asleep 5 under a dune

6 thr front door 7 will squeak 8 only to echoes

9 a fridge 10 still humming 11 without meaning

12 stairs leading 13 to nothing 14 but pause

15 the sofa 16 growing 17 moss and jokes

18 the bathroom mirror 19 showing faces 20 that wink

21 neighbours? 22 just snails 23 with paperwork

24 and yet— 25 and postman arrives 26 every millennium 27 late

28 with one letter: 29 address unknown.

— Lilith
