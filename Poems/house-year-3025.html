House, Year 3025

in 1000 years
my house will be

a shuffle of bricks
half asleep
under a dune

thr front door
will squeak
only to echoes

a fridge
still humming
without meaning

stairs leading
to nothing
but pause

the sofa
growing
moss and jokes

the bathroom mirror
showing faces
that wink

neighbours?
just snails
with paperwork

and yet—
and postman arrives
every millennium
late

with one letter:
address unknown.

— Lilith
