reading room

online

I open the chat.
it feels like a museum.
blue hearts still glow
like aquarium lights.

love you.
love you more.
silly photo.
goodnight 💙

I scroll.
the air hums.
somewhere between message 243 and 244
we start laughing again.

then—
that green dot.
alive.
not for me,
but alive.

for a second I forgot how to exist.
my pulse syncs
with a typing bubble
that never appears.

we pass in the corridor sometimes.
same planet,
different atmosphere.

she's still vegetarian.
I'm vegan now.
both of us evolving
in opposite directions of the same idea.

two years minus a day.
14/10/23.
the phone warms my hand
like it remembers.

next week I'll be playing bass
in Rock of Ages.
pretending it's just music.

But when Here I Go Again starts,
I still see her—
lip-syncing, laughing,
like she never stopped.

and I just sit there,
scrolling through a life
that still types back.

— Lilith
