X.

4 lines · 31 words · 1 min read

Jump to stanza

1 I've never begun with an X before— 2 It never gets picked in the alphabet's draw. 3 But rules must be broken, and boxes unchecked: 4 So X marks this poem, absurd and direct.

— Lilith
