Technoblade Never Dies

Jump to stanza

1 I. The Crowned Pig Rises 2 The world loaded in. 3 He spawned: 4 pig, crown, sword, purpose. 5 A wooden blade, 6 a hunger for victory, 7 and a laugh that knew 8 exactly what it was doing. 9 "Blood for the Blood God," 10 he said— 11 half bit, half battle cry. 12 No mercy. 13 No brakes. 14 Just Technoblade, 15 and the scoreboard.

16 II. Potato King 17 He farmed while we slept. 18 Tilled SkyBlock's soil 19 into empire. 20 Built spreadsheets 21 with more ambition 22 than some nations. 23 It wasn't just harvest— 24 it was war. 25 Every minion slot 26 a soldier, 27 every click 28 a declaration. 29 He wanted all the potatoes. 30 Not for vanity. 31 For the bit. 32 For the win. 33 For the bit. 34 "Technoblade never dies," 35 wasn't bravado. 36 It was prophecy.

37 III. The Blade Unsheathed 38 He broke BedWars— 39 1,400 wins, 40 zero defeats. 41 He bridged with a steering wheel 42 just to prove 43 that skill isn't hardware, 44 it's Technoblade. 45 He dismantled Dream 46 with five hearts left, 47 paused mid-duel 48 to make a joke, 49 then landed the final blow 50 like punctuation. 51 He was myth 52 in disguise. 53 The final boss 54 with a sense of timing 55 and terrible puns.

56 IV. The Exit 57 When the fight came offline, 58 he faced it 59 like every duel— 60 strategic, 61 deadpan, 62 two steps ahead. 63 In where i've been, 64 he told us— 65 IV in arm, 66 grin sharp as ever— 67 that cancer 68 was just 69 the next boss fight. 70 And when the screen 71 faded to his father's voice, 72 and the title read 73 so long nerds, 74 he understood: 75 He didn't lose. 76 He just wrote 77 his own ending.

78 V. Respawn 79 Now, the lobbies are quieter. 80 The throne is empty, 81 but the crown remains. 82 In every SkyBlock grind, 83 every duel montage, 84 every whispered 85 "Technoblade never dies" 86 before the first strike— 87 he lives. 88 Not just in memory, 89 but in muscle memory. 90 In clicks per second. 91 In the silence 92 before the crit. 93 He didn't die. 94 He just reached 95 the next lobby. — Lilith
