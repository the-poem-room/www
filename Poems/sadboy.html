SADBOY

Jump to stanza

1 Eva, can I see bees in a cave?

2 Madam, in Eden, I’m Adam.

3 Step on no pets.

4 A Santa lived as a devil at NASA.

5 No lemon, no melon.

6 Was it a car or a cat I saw?

7 Never odd or even.

8 Yo, Banana Boy!

9 Mr. Owl ate my metal worm.

10 Go hang a salami, I’m a lasagna hog.

— Lilith
