She Said Men Only Want One Thing

(but then rewatched Bridgerton twice)

53 lines · 206 words · 3 min read

1 She said, 2 "Men are animals." 3 As she paused Pride and Prejudice 4 to sigh at Darcy's wet shirt. 5 Said I only want sex— 6 while clutching a candle 7 named Sultry Nights 8 and dreaming up a chapter 9 where the brooding assassin kisses her neck 10 before asking about her trauma.

11 She said, 12 "Porn is for men." 13 Then bit her lip at a vampire 14 saying "You smell like home." 15 Her bookshelf groaned beneath 16 The Billionaire's Baby Secret 17 and Touch Me Until I Heal.

18 She said, 19 "You just want to conquer me." 20 As if I'm the knight 21 and she's the castle. 22 But she's the one 23 who built the tower 24 and whispered 25 "Come save me, stud."

26 Funny, 27 I wanted 28 slow mornings, 29 shared playlists, 30 hands on backs in silence. 31 She want 32 dripping wax, 33 breathless moans, 34 and to be thrown against the wall 35 in the rain.

36 She said, 37 "Men see women as objects." 38 While she photoshopped herself 39 for the algorithm's lust. 40 Built her worth 41 from double taps and corsets, 42 called herself a prize 43 and then blamed me for the hunt.

44 Truth is, 45 not all men are dogs. 46 Some of us 47 are too busy holding space 48 to bark. 49 Too busy wondering 50 how to be enough 51 in a world that only sees us 52 when we perform 53 desire.

— Lilith
