Glitch in the Static

25 lines · 104 words · 2 min read

Jump to stanza

1 Statistically, 2 I shouldn't be here. 3 It's harder to make a whole universe 4 than one stray mind in the dark. 5 Given infinite time, 6 brains like mine 7 outnumber planets.

8 And yet here I am, 9 trying to remember 10 if I left the light on in the kitchen 11 before the cold claims its final flicker.

12 Somewhere, 13 after infinity's worth of disorder, 14 entropy has stumbled into 15 this exact thought: 16 me, 17 half-remembering my own name, 18 counting the cracks in the pavement.

19 It's all noise— 20 every life just a glitch in the static— 21 but mine wasn't even supposed to tune in: 22 an accident, 23 of an accident, 24 of an accident.

25 Seems fine.

— Lilith
