Draft

1 This is a love poem. 2 There is no you. 3 There is only the space 4 where a you might have stood, 5 like a chair pulled out 6 and never pushed back in.

7 This is a protest poem. 8 No one is marching. 9 The signs are all internal, 10 written in pencil, 11 held up only 12 when I'm alone.

13 This is a joke poem. 14 You're allowed to laugh here. 15 I circled the punchline in red 16 and still waited 17 for permission.

18 This is a children's poem. 19 It uses short lines 20 and simple words 21 and asks questions 22 grown-ups step around 23 like cracks in the pavement.

24 This is a religious poem. 25 There is faith in it, 26 but no god. 27 Only the quiet hope 28 that meaning exists 29 even when no one is watching.

30 This is a philosophical poem. 31 Like a mirror 32 trying to explain 33 its reflection.

34 This is not a poem. 35 This is what happens 36 when I keep being asked 37 to choose a box 38 and all of them feel 39 too small.

40 This is a poem. 41 It just isn't finished 42 deciding 43 who it's allowed to be.

— Lilith
