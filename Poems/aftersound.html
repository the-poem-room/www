reading room

aftersound

1 sometimes i forget 2 the shape of your voice 3 how silence 4 learned to sound like you

5 the air between us 6 has stretched so thin 7 it hums

8 sometimes the distance 9 outgrows the body 10 and what was once 11 a voice 12 becomes aftersound

13 i try to remember 14 how the air felt 15 when you were near 16 the texture of your eyes 17 how light used to rest there 18 like a hand on water

19 but months pile quietly 20 like dust on a table 21 and the room kept breathing 22 as time does its slow work 23 softening the edges

24 you become less person 25 more echo 26 until touch is a rumour 27 and names are only weather

28 i think of you 29 and it feels like thinking 30 of a place that still stands 31 but only in memory's map

32 the walls are still there 33 in my mind 34 but the paint flakes 35 the windows blur

36 and still 37 on certain nights 38 you flicker 39 like breath against glass 40 and for a moment

41 i almost believe 42 in form again

43 — Lilith
