Mind Dust

Jump to stanza

1 Thought is unsettled mind dust, 2 restless in its shimmer, 3 swirling through the dark between selves. 4 When it settles, it becomes feeling— 5 a brief constellation of calm. 6 But feelings, left to drift alone, 7 stir the dust once more— 8 each grain naming itself, 9 calling the others other. 10 And so thought returns, 11 turbulent, tender, 12 a storm inside the stillness.

— Lilith
