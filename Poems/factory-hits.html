reading room

Factory Hits

1 The crowd swears Taylor bled it out herself, 2 each comma wrung from heartbreak. 3 Theh crowd swears Ed hummed his hook in the shower, 4 and the shampoo bottle clapped.

5 But really— 6 it's ten Swedes in a windowless room, 7 arguing whether "oh-oh-oh" 8 should come before or after "baby." 9 There's a spreadsheet for syllables. 10 There's a quota for metaphors. 11 There's a dartboard labeled bridge.

12 Swift don't know 13 what you looked and made her do. 14 Ed don't know 15 he's in love with the shape of you. 16 (He's just signed off the demo, 17 smiled, and cashed the check.)

18 And you— 19 you clutch your chest, 20 as if the chorus had your name in it. 21 As if Selena really texted you back. 22 As if Harry actually stayed up 23 writing rhymes for your breakup.

24 It's fine. 25 Keep dancing. 26 The machine thanks you kindly— 27 your streaming tears, 28 your streaming plays, 29 your streaming dollars.

30 Because even if the stars 31 never touched the pen, 32 they still know one thing: 33 you'll sing every word 34 like it was carved on their diary door.

35 — Lilith
