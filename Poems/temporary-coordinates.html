reading room

Temporary Coördinates

95 lines · 337 words · 4 min read

Jump to stanza

1 I'm autistic because I struggle with 2 is not confession, 3 not diagnosis, 4 not essence.

5 It is a coördinate.

6 A graph-point. 7 A pencil dot. 8 where something infinite was happening.

9 We collapse family resemblances— 10 buzzing fluorescent supermarkets, 11 the nylon hiss of a collar tag, 12 sound that lands like metal, 13 light that bruises the eyes— 14 into syllables 15 because language is a small suitcase 16 and experience is high-resolution.

17 "I struggle with sound 18 because I'm autistic" 19 and 20 "I'm autistic 21 so I struggle with sound" 22 look identical at a distance.

23 Both point at the same dot. 24 Both survive small talk.

25 Only one preserves the order.

26 One flows left:

27 word → wound

28 It says: 29 first the name, 30 then the pain.

31 The truer sentence moves right:

32 wound → word

33 Sound as blade. 34 Light as shout. 35 Fabric is fire. 36 Time as a strobe that won't sync.

37 Not: 38 I am overwhelmed because I am autistic.

39 But: 40 I am autistic because the world arrives 41 too loud, 42 too bright, 43 too unbuffered.

44 Noun: autistic 45 Still.

46 Verb: noticing until the room shows its cracks.

47 Noun: gender. 48 Still.

49 Verb: becoming despite the grid.

50 Noun: race. 51 Still.

52 Verb: surviving systems that prefer stillness.

53 Forms prefer nouns. 54 Because verbs move too much. 55 Systems love fixed points. 56 They draw axes through us: 57 functional / broken 58 normal / other 59 visible / erased

60 But a point 61 is supposed 62 to move.

63 I don't struggle with honesty 64 because I'm autistic.

65 I don't struggle with patterns, 66 with depth, 67 with the small fractures in large structures.

68 "I don't struggle... 69 because I'm autistic" 70 is not denial.

71 It is calibration.

72 A reminder: 73 maps are not territory. 74 Coördinates are not cages.

75 We turn constellations into dots 76 for speed, 77 for safety, 78 for being understood 79 before the bell rings.

80 This is not essence. 81 This is transport.

82 A storm folded small enough 83 to cross a room. 84 A weather system 85 flattened into breath.

86 I am not fixed. 87 I am not pinned.

88 I am a moving coördinate 89 misfiled as a noun, 90 sliding across invisible axes, 91 more verb than word, 92 more weather than label.

93 A storm that did not agree 94 but learned 95 how to be spelled.

— Lilith
