Make Him a Sandwich (Episode 69)

Hello and welcome
to the 69th episode
of the Make Him a Sandwich podcast,

hosted by popular conservative pundit
Lilith Foxcroft —
which is really funny,
because I'm definitely not conservative,

But I do own a bowtie
and I once got recommended
a Jordan Peterson video,
so I think that makes me qualified.

Now, today's very important topic is
why society collapsed
the moment women were allowed opinions.

But before that,
a word from our sponsor.

This episode is brought to you by
Traditional Values™,
the only supplement that restores your masculinity
by simply shouting
"facts don't care about your feelings"
repeatedly into a mirror.

Side effects may include:
baldness,
a podcast microphone,
and a sudden desire
to debate 17-year-olds on TikTok.

Now, I've been doing some serious
intellectual thinking,

and by that I mean
scrolling Twitter at 3am
while drinking something called
Patriot Energy Drink
in a flavour called
Gunsmoke Berry.

And I've come to a bold conclusion.

Men
are under
attack.

Yes.
You heard me.
Under.
Attack.

Every time I see a woman in the workplace,
I personally lose three IQ points
and a small but spiritually significant
amount of chest hair.

Coincidence?

I think not.

And don't even get me started
on vegans.

These people wake up and think,
"You know what this world needs?
Less steak
and more vibes."

Back in my day,
real men ate meat,
drove straight vehicles,
and never, ever spoke
to their children
about emotions.

In today's episode,
we're also going to bring on
our guest expert —

a man who once read
the back of a philosophy book
and now calls himself
a "free thinker."

Please welcome...

Brad.

— Lilith
