Make Him a Sandwich (Episode 69)

Jump to stanza

1 Hello and welcome 2 to the 69th episode 3 of the Make Him a Sandwich podcast,

4 hosted by popular conservative pundit 5 Lilith Foxcroft — 6 which is really funny, 7 because I'm definitely not conservative,

8 But I do own a bowtie 9 and I once got recommended 10 a Jordan Peterson video, 11 so I think that makes me qualified.

12 Now, today's very important topic is 13 why society collapsed 14 the moment women were allowed opinions.

15 But before that, 16 a word from our sponsor.

17 This episode is brought to you by 18 Traditional Values™, 19 the only supplement that restores your masculinity 20 by simply shouting 21 "facts don't care about your feelings" 22 repeatedly into a mirror.

23 Side effects may include: 24 baldness, 25 a podcast microphone, 26 and a sudden desire 27 to debate 17-year-olds on TikTok.

28 Now, I've been doing some serious 29 intellectual thinking,

30 and by that I mean 31 scrolling Twitter at 3am 32 while drinking something called 33 Patriot Energy Drink 34 in a flavour called 35 Gunsmoke Berry.

36 And I've come to a bold conclusion.

37 Men 38 are under 39 attack.

40 Yes. 41 You heard me. 42 Under. 43 Attack.

44 Every time I see a woman in the workplace, 45 I personally lose three IQ points 46 and a small but spiritually significant 47 amount of chest hair.

48 Coincidence?

49 I think not.

50 And don't even get me started 51 on vegans.

52 These people wake up and think, 53 "You know what this world needs? 54 Less steak 55 and more vibes."

56 Back in my day, 57 real men ate meat, 58 drove straight vehicles, 59 and never, ever spoke 60 to their children 61 about emotions.

62 In today's episode, 63 we're also going to bring on 64 our guest expert —

65 a man who once read 66 the back of a philosophy book 67 and now calls himself 68 a "free thinker."

69 Please welcome...

70 Brad.

— Lilith
