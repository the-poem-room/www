You Live in Colour, I Dream in Green

Jump to stanza

1 You live in colour— 2 sunset hues etched upon your skin, 3 a canvas vibrant, endless, loud.

4 I dream in green, 5 a meadow I visit 6 only when music plays— 7 soft moss beneath my feet, 8 the hum of leaves 9 like whispered chords.

10 Your lips glossed with ultraviolet light, 11 dancing in neon beats, 12 while I breathe chlorophyll dreams, 13 rooted in silence, 14 growing in shadows.

15 For one moment 16 of stillness— 17 in a world 18 too loud

19 And I, 20 I was born 21 with chlorophyll in my lungs 22 with melodies woven 23 through my veins, 24 a song in every breath.

25 I flinch—

26 Not from you— 27 but from the fear 28 you'll pull me out 29 before I'm ready 30 to wake from this dream

— Lilith
