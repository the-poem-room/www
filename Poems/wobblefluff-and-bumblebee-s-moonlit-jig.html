Wobblefluff and Bumblebee's Moonlit Jig

12 lines · 77 words · 1 min read

Jump to stanza

1 In the pickle-patch beneath violet skies, 2 Wobblefluff wobbled on warm spaghetti pies. 3 Bumblebee buzzed in a tutu of cheese, 4 Tickling tulips with slippery ease.

5 The clock tick-tocked in jellybean time, 6 While marmalade clouds sang backwards in rhyme. 7 Zigzagging zebras wore polka-dot hats, 8 Playing hopscotch with juggling cats.

9 A whispering waffle sailed past the moon, 10 Humming a tune to a hummingfish croon. 11 I laughed with a grin and a gleaming spoon, 12 As noise spun like a loony balloon.

— Lilith
