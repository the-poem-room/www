How I Stayed Still

1 today, 2 i 3 couldn't 4 get 5 out 6 of 7 bed — 8 the sheet 9 cold 10 as 11 thought

12 no why 13 came near

14 soft light 15 stayed still

16 time kept still 17 hands kep still 18 me kept still

19 dreams made mud 20 thoughts made knots 21 mind made mist

22 if all moves 23 by dumb cause 24 then i move too

25 still i breathe 26 still i think 27 still i wait

28 i like quiet 29 don't like crowds 30 but that shifts

31 some things hurt 32 and i stay 33 some don't 34 and i leave

35 i hate school 36 but learn well 37 at home 38 where thought 39 breathes freely

40 the world teaches 41 speed and noise, 42 asks me to act 43 without awareness, 44 to answer, not think, 45 not move, not notice.

46 it fills the hours 47 with useful motions, 48 grades and smiles, 49 proof and purpose — 50 and leaves no room 51 to look inside.

52 but i want 53 to see clearly, 54 to think slow, 55 to stay awake, 56 to live in rhythm, 57 not reaction.

58 i go running 59 though i ache 60 i eat kindly 61 because i care, 62 my reasons repeat 63 until they sound 64 like instincts.

65 pleasure and reason 66 pull different ways 67 and still meet.

68 freedom, maybe, 69 is rhythm we notice — 70 habit mistaken 71 for song.

72 Six Attempts at Freedom

73 six heartbeats, passing, 74 like steady logic, 75 a system that works 76 without needing belief.

77 six breaths tremble, 78 half will, half wind, 79 obedience mistaken 80 for calm.

81 six seconds linger 82 where thought hovers 83 before it begins again; 84 i like that hesitation.

85 six shadows move 86 across my wall, 87 versions of me 88 rehearsing mornings 89 already lived.

90 six words whisper, 91 you are still here now. 92 the grammar shifts, 93 but the meaning holds.

94 six lights fall 95 through the curtain's edge, 96 measuring the moment 97 without caring for it. 98 they rest on my hand, 99 and i rest too.

100 the room stays still, 101 the air hums faintly, 102 six lights fade softly, 103 still i breathe, 104 still i think, 105 still i wait, 106 and the world, 107 for all its noise, 108 cannot touch 109 this quiet.

110 — Lilith
